About Alice

UX/UI Designer, Fullstack Developer.
A proud Jack of All Trades, and Master of Metaphors

In the past the industrial revolution defined our values. The assembly line praised specialization. "Jack of all trades; master of none" was one saying that resonated with the values of the past century. As a jack, I can tell you that we are all masters of one skill: learning

Connections and patterns are my main tools for problem solving. I translate data (from user interviews, competitive analysis, etc.) into abstract concepts (user painpoints). I delight in converting abstract problems into tangible solutions (prototypes and products).

When I am not solving problems, I like to: run outdoors, dive into in a character-driven series, and listen to friends talk through the night as we watch the sunrise together.
